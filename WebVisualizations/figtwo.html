Humidity

This plot shows that humidity occurs above, at, and below the equator without direct correlation. A majority of the humidity rates tend to be greater than 60% across all latitude points. There is very low to no correlation of humidity between northern and southern hemispheres. The lowest points of humidity occur in the furthest tip of the northern hemisphere.
