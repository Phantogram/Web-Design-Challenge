Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this analysis is to answer questions regarding the weather as it relates to the proximity of the equator. A dataset of over 500 cites was pulled from OpenWeatherMap API. A table including those cities can be found on the "Data" tab.

After gathering and cleaning up the data, Matplotlib was used to plot weather aspects against latitude points. The weather aspects that we analyzed include: maximum temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed. This site showcases the visualizations derived from the data. Each visualization is paired with a description and explanation of findings and correlations. On the "Comparison" tab you will find all four visualizations.

This analysis will allow you to make improved decisions about weather near the equator. You might even use this data to plan a vacation given your preferred weather measurements.
